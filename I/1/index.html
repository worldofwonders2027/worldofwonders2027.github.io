There are three principles by which these systems run:

1. "Everything is awesome."

This means that the way to rule is by cooperation.

By inspiration - not by inspiring limits.

2. "Everything is connected to everything else, either directly, or indirectly."

This means that everything is in an environment. In terms of the Internet and world this means we will focus a lot on the interfaces for these environments - how we are inspiring people.

3. "Everything is possible."

And, what are we inspiring them to do - of course - anything they dream of!

Under the third principle fall all ideas intended to allow everyone's dreams to come true, which we listened to in principle one, listening to/inspiring everyone, and then offering them an environment in principle two.
