An alternative approach to idol concerts that I will suggest to folks that don't like just being idol-like is the approach of showing your virtual body - or physical body - it's your secret model...

And, just going around the stage, doing flips, doing crazy things, etc. while singing and dancing!

You can sing any song, or do anything, also.

For example, I will propose, for people that want to be unconventional, that you go ahead and sing Rucka Rucka Ali's songs.

I have loved the man since 2020 - beautiful songs - they're all about the subjective approach you have to them - not just the words - I have found.

They lend themselves sooo well to interpretation and play!


Marina also has some beautiful songs, just as does Rave The Requiem, whose Stigmata Itch album is brilliant.

Or maybe you would like to sing video game OST music, like music from Kirby, or Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of The New World, or Tales of Symphonia, or the PokePark games (Where is PokePark 3?!) - all games I love.

Or maybe you would like music from other cultures, such as Armonian's beautiful works of art mixing Western culture with Indian music and language.

Let us also talk about all the anime songs you could sing on stage - I don't care about copyright - There is no such thing as "no" until you accept it as a no.


Or maybe you would like to sing Jreg's amazing songs, or other influencer music, or maybe you would like to just create a meditative or ASMR environment for your audience on stage!

Spontaneity can give you better results than thousands of years of planning if you can learn to love this moment deeper than your next breath, and from there enjoy it.
