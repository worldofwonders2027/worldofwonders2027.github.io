I would love to conduct a study of the oil industry, seeing if oil truly has to be harmful to nature.

If it is harmful, I will discontinue working with it, but if it isn't, I'd love to look deeper into it's uses.

I don't like electric cars, for the record - the pollution created in making a battery is often half of that which a gas car will have done in it's entire life, and let's not forget that there needs to be work to get that electricity, as with the fossil fuel.

This is just an idea, and it illustrates the power of helping people who know what they're doing do what they know and be known to the public.
